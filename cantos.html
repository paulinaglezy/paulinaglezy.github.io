Physical Installation

A dark and empty room in which a melody is reproduced through speakers while a video is shown on the walls. The melody was designed so that one second of the sound piece equals one day in real time. Each musical note represents an entire animal species that has become extinct according to real statistics.

Installation shown in the documentary later on


Miniseries “Non-Human Stories in the Anthropocene”

3 videos in which archive footage of videos and sounds were edited to show the stories of 3 animal species in danger of extinction.
The miniseries aims to have a conceptual-poetic visual treatment.
